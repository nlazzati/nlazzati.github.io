Here is my CV.

I am an Associate Professor at the Department of Economics of UC Santa Cruz.

I am also an Associate Editor of the Journal of Economic Behavior & Organization (JEBO) and the Journal of Mathematical Economics (JME).

I am a microeconomic theorist interested in integrating theory and data. Much of my research focuses on games of strategic complements ---often called supermodular games.

I am currently developing an education hub platform that aims to support an active learning-by-doing approach.
