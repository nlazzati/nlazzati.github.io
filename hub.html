Prof. Kristian Lopez-Vargas and I are currently developing an education hub platform.

The project consists on an online platform that aims to support an active learning approach for many courses.

For each course in the education hub the platform will offer an online book that the faculty can personalize.

The online book is envisioned to have interactive pictures, questions that can be checked for immediate feedback, and the possibility for the students to create new questions!

The online platfom will save information on the interaction of the student with the online book. We hope this information will facilitate targeted instruction.

This project started during the pandemics when I was teaching Uma kinder-math by using online platforms.
